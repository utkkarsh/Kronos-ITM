CODE RELAY

Code-Relay is a purely coding based event in which participants could use any platform and any language in which they feel comfortable to code. Each participant will be given the first question and asked to write a code and get the output, on finishing the question the participant will be asked the next question in which the output of previous question will be used as a compulsory hint for the next question without which the next question could not be solved. The difficulty level of the questions will increase gradually, and the hints from the outputs will become tougher to crack.

MOOT COURT

Mooting is the oral Presentation of a legal issue or problem against an opposing counsel or before a judge. It is a practical method to give actual court like experience to a law student still studying in Law University. While participating in a moot court a law student learns the framing of a legal case, its preparation and presentation in a Court of Law. The whole developing process of a legal case from framing of issues to legal argument before Judge melts into the conscience of a young law student and develop a bright and successful legal practitioner over the academic period. The moot court competitions in general involve participation of law students enrolled in Law colleges and law universities. It comprises of two levels vis., Written Submissions and Oral Presentations. The participating team in general comprise of two speakers and one researcher.
